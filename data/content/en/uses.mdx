This is a summary of the software/hardware setup that I use on a daily basis.

## Editor + Terminal

- [Google Antigravity](https://antigravity.google/).
- [cmux](https://www.cmux.dev/) is my terminal of choice, it's very helpful to use it in these AI times.
- I use [psudoFont Liga Mono](https://github.com/psudo-dev/psudofont-liga-mono) as my default editor font.

## Desktop Apps

- [Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) is my browser.
- I use [1Password](https://1password.com/) to protect my passwords.
- I keep track of my ToDos and project notes in [Notion](https://www.notion.so/).
- [Toggl](https://toggl.com/) makes easier to track my freelance timesheets.
- When I need to design something I use [Affinity](https://www.affinity.studio/), but I also use [Illustrator](https://www.adobe.com/products/illustrator.html) or [Photoshop](https://www.adobe.com/products/photoshop.html).
- I have several Google and G Suite accounts, so to centralize the use of email I use [Mimestream](https://mimestream.com/).

- [Apple AirPods 2nd gen](https://www.apple.com/airpods-2nd-generation/) wireless headphones and [Sony WH-H910N](https://www.sony.com/electronics/headband-headphones/wh-h910n).
